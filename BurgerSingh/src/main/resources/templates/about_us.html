About Us

Great Food Comes First

Burger Singh is known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1954, Burger Singh is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for 70 successful years.
